The Packet Handler

  • Split into 3 parts:

    • Receiver: accepts packets, stamps them with the time and places them in a queue.
    • Arbitrator: dequeues packets and decides what to do with them.
      • If they should be delayed, puts them in a time-sorted PQ for the Releaser to handle.
    • Releaser: releases delayed packets.
